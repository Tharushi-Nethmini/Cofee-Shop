Our History

Bean Haven Cafe was founded in 2010 by childhood friends Emma and Jack, inspired by their love for coffee and global travels. They envisioned a cozy spot where people could enjoy high-quality, ethically sourced coffee.

Starting small, Bean Haven Cafe quickly became a local favorite. Emma and Jack perfected their brewing techniques and built strong relationships with global coffee growers, ensuring top-quality beans and supporting fair trade. The menu soon included homemade pastries, sandwiches, and seasonal specialties.

Today, Bean Haven Cafe is a cherished community hub. Emma and Jack's commitment to quality and a welcoming atmosphere remains at its core, celebrating the art of coffee and the joy of gathering.
